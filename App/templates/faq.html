Frequently Asked Questions

You can make a booking by selecting a property, choosing your dates, and clicking "Book Now."

Use the "Support" page to fill out a contact form or browse our FAQ section for quick answers.

You can cancel a booking by visiting your "My Bookings" page and selecting the cancellation option.

If a host cancels your booking, you will receive a full refund or assistance in finding an alternative property.

After completing your stay, you can leave a review by visiting the "My Bookings" page and selecting "Write Review."

Go to the "Profile" page to update your information such as email, phone number, and password.

Currently, payments must be made in full. We do not support installments at this time.

We accept credit/debit cards, mobile payments, and bank transfers.
